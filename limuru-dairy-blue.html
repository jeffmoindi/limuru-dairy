Our farms

Highland ground makes different milk

Limuru sits over 2,200 metres up, where cool nights and volcanic soil grow grass most lowland herds never taste. Our eighteen partner farms are all within a 20-kilometre radius of our dairy — close enough that milk is chilled before it's even left the parish.

We've kept the same route since 1994: collect at dawn, chill by seven, box by nine. No powder, no imports, no long-life shortcuts.

"You can taste the season in it — grass in April, something greener by July." — Mama Wanjiru, dairy farmer, Tigoni
